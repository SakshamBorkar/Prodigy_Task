CHIEF DESSIGN OFFICER
at citizenM

Claudia Abt built a career as an architect with
one of Germany’s largest architectural firms
and spent several years running her own small
studio. Currently serving as Chief Design
Officer at citizenM hotels, she oversees design
and space planning for new hotel
developments across the EU, US, and Asia. We
will discuss:

  • How to move from one design speciality to looking after multiple design departments

  • How to generate upscale revenue through bold and fun brand personality
  • How to grow to Chief Design Officer position from background in architecture
